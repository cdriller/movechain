# Problem
- semester ticket
- different app for every operator
- super app -> different prices (other cities)
- phone dead / offline

# Solution
- interface -> operators sign in once and then don't have anything more to do, riders can buy at any operator with just one wallet and not different apps
- prices saved on-chain -> transparent
- prices fix, not increased manually -> just change with ETH-price -> idea of blockchain (no monopoly power for the operators)

# benefits
- just hop in and ride
- not carry paper tickets or download apps (storage)
- completely offline
- only buying credits online, can also be done offline by vending machines at stations
- operator pays gas -> no hidden costs
